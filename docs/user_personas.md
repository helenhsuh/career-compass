# User Personas

Career Compass is designed to support users across life stages and with diverse constraints. These personas guide feature design and ethical considerations.

---

## Persona 1: Early-Career Explorer
**Age:** 18–25  
**Needs:** clarity, confidence, understanding of transferable skills  
**Constraints:** limited experience, financial pressure, uncertainty  
**Goals:** identify aligned roles, understand learning paths

---

## Persona 2: Mid-Career Pivoting Professional
**Age:** 30–50  
**Needs:** stability, meaningful work, skill translation  
**Constraints:** dependents, caregiving responsibilities, relocation limits  
**Goals:** find realistic pathways that honor both career and life demands

---

## Persona 3: Caregiver Balancing Multiple Roles
**Age:** 28–55  
**Needs:** flexibility, remote options, predictable schedules  
**Constraints:** caregiving, limited time for upskilling  
**Goals:** identify roles that fit lifestyle constraints

---

## Persona 4: Burnout Recovery or Career Reset
**Age:** 25–45  
**Needs:** emotional safety, gradual re-entry, confidence rebuilding  
**Constraints:** mental bandwidth, financial considerations  
**Goals:** explore aligned roles without overwhelm

---

These personas ensure the system remains inclusive, empathetic, and grounded in real-world needs.
